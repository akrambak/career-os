Lead with the bet. The link goes at the bottom, NOT the first line (LinkedIn's
algorithm de-prioritizes posts that start with external links).

## Why this works

- **Hook in the first line:** the seniority claim before any AI mention. Recruiters and hiring managers scan for that.
- **Concrete deliverable list:** four bullets, four specific things shipped. No "I'm exploring..." language.
- **Open-source + MIT:** signals trustworthiness for both recruiters and clients.
- **The ask is dual-channel:** FT and freelance in the same sentence — anyone reading either way feels addressed.
- **Hashtags last:** required for reach, but they read as metadata at the end.

## When to post

- Publish on a Tuesday or Wednesday between 8–10am CEST
- Do NOT comment on your own post for the first 30 min (LinkedIn algo)
- Reply to every comment within 4 hours of posting — engagement boosts reach
